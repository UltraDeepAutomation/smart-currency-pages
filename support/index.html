Smart Currency Support

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Smart Currency is a focused iPhone exchange-rate utility for quick fiat and top-crypto conversions with source freshness and local widgets.

What The App Does

  • Converts one base amount into multiple target currencies.
  • Shows fiat and top-crypto reference rates from public sources.
  • Keeps saved conversion state locally on device.
  • Preserves cached rates for offline or degraded provider states.
  • Mirrors saved state into WidgetKit widgets.

What The App Does Not Do

  • No wallet, custody, deposits, withdrawals, or payments.
  • No trading, order placement, brokerage, tax, or portfolio PnL workflow.
  • No financial advice.
  • No accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking, or social feed.

Rate Sources

Current public sources are European Central Bank, Frankfurter, and CoinGecko. Rates may be delayed, unavailable, stale, or temporarily rate-limited by providers. The app displays source/freshness state and keeps last-known local cached rates when a provider is unavailable.

Troubleshooting

If rates do not update immediately:

  1. Check your network connection.
  2. Wait for the refresh cadence: fiat rates refresh at most every 5 minutes (At most every 5 minutes), and crypto rates refresh at most every 10 minutes (At most every 10 minutes).
  3. Reopen the app if iOS paused background activity.
  4. If widgets appear stale, open the main app once so it can refresh and save the latest local snapshot.
