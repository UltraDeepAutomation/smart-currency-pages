Smart Currency Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Smart Currency is a local-first exchange-rate utility. The app is designed to show currency and top-crypto reference rates without accounts, advertising, tracking, analytics, or user profiling.

Data We Do Not Collect

Smart Currency does not collect personal data from the app.

The app does not require an account, name, email address, phone number, payment information, contact list, photos, camera, microphone, precise location, advertising identifier, or broker/wallet credentials.

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, tracking SDKs, remote-config SDKs, or social SDKs.

Local App Data

Smart Currency stores your selected currencies, saved conversion pairs, amount preferences, appearance preference, accent color, number precision, and cached rate results locally on your device.

This local data is used only to make the app and its widgets work. Smart Currency does not transmit this saved portfolio data to the developer.

Exchange-Rate Requests

Smart Currency requests public reference rates from external rate providers so it can display current exchange-rate information. Current public sources are:

  • European Central Bank reference rates
  • Frankfurter fiat rates
  • CoinGecko crypto prices

These requests contain the currencies needed for a rate lookup. They do not include your name, account, contact details, payment data, saved portfolio file, or entered conversion amount.

External providers may receive standard network information required to serve an internet request, such as IP address and request metadata, under their own privacy practices. Smart Currency does not use those requests for advertising, tracking, analytics, or user profiling.

Widgets

Smart Currency widgets read the same local app data stored on your device. Widgets do not fetch live providers directly and do not collect personal data.

Children

Smart Currency is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If Smart Currency adds accounts, analytics, crash reporting, payments, wallet/broker integrations, remote configuration, or any other feature that changes data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy answers must be updated before release.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the Smart Currency support page published on the App Store product page: https://ultradeepautomation.github.io/smart-currency-pages/support/
